Discover Stories

Dr. Romeo Jacob

Dental Professional Volunteer

"Every dental mission will yield a totally different experience."

Dental Mission is not new to me and every dental mission will yield a totally different experience. Every dental mission will always be an eye opener as to how much we need to promote awareness on the importance of oral health. It will always be a validation that the dental health situation is an issue that needs to be addressed. Patients shows a significant number of decayed teeth in the mouth beyond repair not to mention the degree of gum problems affecting each individual. Those people afflicted are the poor masses that are the marginalized group. It is therfore necessary that awareness must be promoted as to the importance of tooth brushing or oral health education in general. Poverty alleviation is one key to bring solution to this lingering issues.

About Us

Ang IPINasaya ay galing sa salitang, “ipin” at “saya”, na nangangahulugang masayang ngipin. Ang layunin ng website na ito ay matulungan ang mga Pilipino na mapanatiling malinis ang kanilang mga ngipin at makamit ang pagkakaroon ng perpekto at masasayang mga ngiti. Sa pamamagitan ng website, makatutulong ito sa pag-promote ng dental health sa mga Pilipino sa paraan na mas mapapadali nitong maipamahagi ang mga impormasyon tungkol sa basic oral health care. Maaari itong gamitin ng mga non-government organizations na magsasagawa ng mga dental missions at turuan ang mga komunidad na pagbibigyan nila ng serbisyo.
